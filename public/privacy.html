Privacy

How Word Cube stores progress and measures simple site activity.

What stays on your device

Word Cube stores your current puzzle progress in your browser so a refresh does not wipe your run. That includes the active cube state, score, found words, hint state, and the current daily puzzle label.

Site analytics

Word Cube uses Cloudflare Web Analytics to understand visits, rough traffic patterns, and site performance. This helps track whether the site is loading properly and how much it is being used.

What Word Cube does not ask for

Word Cube does not require an account, does not ask for payment details, and does not ask for your name or email address to play.

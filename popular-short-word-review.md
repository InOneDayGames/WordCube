# Popular Short Word Review

This review focuses on 4- and 5-letter entries in `public/popular.txt`.

The goal is not to remove rude words or long words. The problem is short words that are legal but do not feel genuinely popular, because those are easy for players to stumble into and can make the red-highlight affordance feel like dictionary fishing.

Review process:

- Work letter by letter.
- Mark candidates as `likely cut from popular` or `maybe`.
- Remove words only from `public/popular.txt` if we decide to prune them. Leave `public/dictionary.txt` alone unless we make a separate decision about legal words.
